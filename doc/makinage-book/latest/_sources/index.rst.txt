The Maki Nage Book
====================

Welcome to the documentation of Maki Nage. From an initial discovery up to
advanced topics, this content is organized as a handson followed by specific
topics. If you are new to Maki Nage, you are encouraged to read the chapters in
order, at least up to the handson one. People already at ease with Maki Nage can
go directly to the other chapters to get information on specific topics.

Note that the complete references of Rxsci operators is available `here
<https://www.makinage.org/doc/rxsci/latest/index.html>`_.
