The Maki Nage Book
====================

Welcome to the documentation of Maki Nage. From an initial discovery up to
advanced topics, this content is organized as a hands-on followed by specific
topics. If you are new to Maki Nage, you are encouraged to read the chapters in
order, at least up to the hands-on one. People already at ease with Maki Nage can
go directly to the other chapters to get information on specific topics.

The complete references of RxSci operators is available `here <https://www.makinage.org/doc/rxsci/latest/index.html>`_.
